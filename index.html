Tianyi Wang

Postdoctoral Researcher, Nanyang Technological University

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Chunyan Miao. My research interests include Deepfake Detection, Misinformation Detection, Multimedia Forensics, and Generative AI.

Before joining NTU, I received my Ph.D. dgree in Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Dr. Kam Pui Chow. I received my double-major B.S. degree in Computer Science and Applied & Computational Mathematical Sciences at University of Washington (UW) in Seattle.
